Project 1: Layout & Hierarchy

For this project we had to choose between two different articles and create three different typographic layouts in total. Each layout had to meet specific requirements regarding the typeface and point size. This project was all about exploring hierarchy and layout in order to create a visually pleasing final deisgn. We could only use black and white and had to use all of the text provided.


Research

The first thing I did during this project was look at typographic poster designs. I used a combination of pinterest and books to complete my initial research.



Thumbnails

Once I had done some research I moved on to paper and began sketching thumbnail designs for each requirement. I tried to play around with the layout and hierarchy and make each design different. I kept each thumbnail as simple as possible so I could try out more designs.


115*207 - One typeface in as many sizes
