Session 10: The Research Journey

Research is a key factor in any project. It allows us to learn around a subject matter to widen our knowledge. You must use relevent sources to help seek answers to questions or solutions to a problem.. Research contributes to the existing body of knowledge on a topic area.


Problem - Research Question - Research Method - Outcome


There are many ways to go about researching for an essay, but these are 6 main steps:
1) Initial ideas
2) Find specific aim
3) Review the literature
4) Pick a research method
5) Carry out the research
6) Write up


Key Methods:
1) Visual
– The study of media and artefacts
– Deconstruction and evaluation of films, comics and graphic novels, games, adverts, or other visual artefact - use theory to frame analysis
– Enables the deconstruction of the visual artefact
2) Social Science
– Approaches used in sociology, cultural studies, etc.
– Quantitative (surveys, counts, questionnaires)
– qualitative (interviews, focus groups, conversations, diaries)
3) Practice Based
– This is where the research method is creative practice
