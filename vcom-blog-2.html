Project 2: Type Activities

Before we begin any project, we are always given mini activities to introduce us to the topic. For this module we were given three starter activities.


Ampersands

The first activity we were given was to collect 20 different ampersands. This introduced us to the various properties of different typefaces. I presented my ampersands in a small book format.



Type Personality

The next activity involved us choosing one of two scripts and then visually communicating it through the choice of typeface. I chose the script from Harry Potter and drew my inspiration from the Robert Massin script book for the Bald Soprano by Eugene Ionesco.



DIY Alphabet

This was the last mini activity we had to complete before our main projects. We were asked to create a visual alphabet from materials we had nearby. I chose to cut mine out of card so that the negative space formed part of the alphabet.
