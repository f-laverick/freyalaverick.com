La Cérémonie

directed by Claude Chabrol, 1995

←

Plot

La Cérémonie tells the story of a young woman, Sophie Bonhomme, who is hired as a maid by the Lelièvre family. Sophie befriends a mercurial postmistress Jeanne, with dire consequences.
